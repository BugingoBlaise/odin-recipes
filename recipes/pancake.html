Pancake Recipes

Sweet/savory hybrids can be tricky sometimes but I think this worked beautifully. We are talking about bacon, cheese, and onions after all, so it wasn't that big of a shock. The way the chipotle-spiced maple syrup brought everything together was a risk well rewarded. Pancakes are fluffy and delicious. They are a great breakfast food, And they can be topped with anything you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Use room temperature ingredients. This will help the batter to come together more easily and produce light and fluffy pancakes.
  2. Don't overmix the batter. Overmixing will develop the gluten in the flour, which will make the pancakes tough.
  3. Cook the pancakes over medium heat. This will help them to cook evenly without burning.
  4. Don't overcrowd the skillet. If you add too many pancakes to the skillet at once, they will steam instead of cook.
  5. Flip the pancakes when they are bubbles begin to form on the surface and the edges are dry.
  6. Serve the pancakes immediately with your favorite toppings
