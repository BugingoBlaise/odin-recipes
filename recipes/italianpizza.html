Italian pizza is a type of pizza that originated in Italy. It is made with a thin, hand-tossed crust that is baked in a wood-fired oven. The pizza is typically topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of other ingredients, such as mushrooms, onions, peppers, and olives.
